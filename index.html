Welcome

This planner will: (a) ask when you start, (b) let you choose your target exam sitting, (c) allocate weekly topics by effort, (d) add revision weeks when there’s time, and (e) show a week-by-week plan you can reorder.

Exam date is treated as the first day of the FLK1 exam week to anchor the plan’s end.
